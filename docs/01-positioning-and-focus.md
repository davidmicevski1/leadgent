# Positioning and Focus (Agency Version)

## 1. Start Narrow

Initial ICP recommendation:
- Founder-led or lean sales teams (5-50 employees)
- Services or SaaS companies with enough deal size to justify outbound
- Teams with some demand proof but weak outbound consistency

Reason:
- A narrow wedge drives faster proof, stronger messaging, and more referrals.

## 2. Core Service Stack

Service A (entry):
- Outbound foundation sprint (targeting, data, messaging, infrastructure)

Service B (core retainer):
- Ongoing outbound execution + weekly optimization

Service C (expansion):
- GTM automation implementation for repetitive manual workflows

## 3. Positioning Statement Draft

"LeadGent is an outbound and GTM execution agency that helps lean B2B teams build consistent pipeline and eliminate manual operations drag."

## 4. Client Qualification Rules

Only accept clients if:
- They have a clear sales objective
- They can define their offer and buyers
- They can support weekly feedback loops

## 5. Avoid Early-Stage Traps

- Custom one-off projects with no repeatability
- Selling broad "automation for anything" too early
- Overbuilding internal tooling before repeatable delivery exists
